3. Device Permissions

  • Microphone: used only during "hold-to-talk" voice input, to capture speech and transcribe it to text.
  • Network: used for sign-in, translation, sync and subscription verification.

Permissions are not enabled by default and can be revoked anytime in system settings. Revoking disables the related feature but not others.

4. Storage

  • Location: personal data we collect is stored on servers located in Singapore.
  • Retention: we retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes stated here, after which we delete or anonymize it unless otherwise required by law.
  • On-device: your sentence and word books are cached in a local device database; login credentials are stored encrypted in the device's secure storage.

5. International Data Transfers

Because our servers and certain third-party providers (such as the LLM providers) may be located outside your country or region, your personal data may be transferred to, and stored or processed in, jurisdictions whose data-protection laws differ from those where you live. We take reasonable measures to ensure such transfers comply with applicable law and that your data receives an adequate level of protection.

6. Sharing, Transfer & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We will not share it except:

  • with your prior explicit consent;
  • providing necessary information to the processors listed in Section 2 to deliver those features;
  • where required by law or by a competent judicial/administrative authority;
  • as necessary to protect the rights of the App and users, or to investigate fraud and security risks.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy involving a transfer of data, we will require the successor to remain bound by this Policy, or seek your consent again.

7. How We Protect Your Information

  • HTTPS encryption in transit; login credentials stored encrypted;
  • access control and least-privilege for server-side data;
  • to provide and improve the service, troubleshoot, and prevent security risks, authorized personnel may access your query content on a minimum-necessary basis; such personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations and limited to these purposes;
  • while we take reasonable measures, no internet transmission is absolutely secure; we continually work to safeguard your data.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & correction: view and edit your account profile and study content in the App;

  • Data portability: where applicable law allows, request a copy of the data you provided to us in a common format;
  • Account deletion: request account deletion in the App's Settings; we will delete or anonymize associated personal data unless otherwise required by law;
  • Withdraw consent: revoke permissions or stop using the related features;
  • Complaints: contact us via "Contact Us" below; if a data-protection authority is established where you live, you may also lodge a complaint with it.

The App is intended primarily for adults. If you are a minor, please read this Policy with a guardian and use the App only with the guardian's consent. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children; if we learn we have done so without prior guardian consent, we will delete it promptly.
